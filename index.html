About Us

SNV Stamping Solutions Pvt Ltd, founded in March 2025, specializes in manufacturing high-precision stators and rotors for electric vehicles, industrial motors, agriculture, and robotics. Our advanced facility is equipped with 220T high-speed presses operating at 440 strokes per minute, enabling us to deliver zero-defect quality with the shortest lead times in the industry. By leveraging the latest technology and automated processes, we ensure consistent precision and reliability in every component we produce.

Guided by Toyota-inspired Lean practices such as TPS, Kaizen, and 5S, SNV focuses on continuous improvement, operational efficiency, and stringent quality control at every stage of production. With a strong commitment to innovation and excellence, we are strategically positioned to serve India’s rapidly growing EV and motor markets, providing world-class stamping solutions that meet the evolving needs of our clients.

Vision

To drive India's leadership in advanced, defect-free stamping solutions for the global electric motor industry, built on lean practices, TPS excellence, and a culture that makes us the best place to work.

Mission

We manufacture a wide range of stator and rotor stampings across multiple sheet thicknesses with the highest precision, uncompromising quality, and the shortest lead times. Our mission is to deliver reliable, innovative, and cost-effective solutions that empower electric mobility, industrial, and energy applications.
