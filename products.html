"High-Performance Stamping with 200 Ton Yadon Press Line"

Optimized for efficiency, durability, and seamless large-scale operations.

This Yadon High-Speed Press Line is a powerful system for high-volume, precise metal stamping. It uses its strength and speed to quickly and accurately form parts from very thin sheets (0.1mm to 1mm), making it ideal for many different products. The facility also has welding equipment specifically for electric motor components (like rotors and stators), allowing for a smooth and efficient transition from making the parts to assembling them. Furthermore, we have an in-house facility to design and build the stamping tools (dies), which guarantees fast customization, perfect quality, and zero defects for everything we produce for the automotive, industrial, and electric motor industries.

"High-Performance Stamping with 125 Ton Yadon Press Line"

Optimized for efficiency, durability, and seamless large-scale operations.

The 125-ton Yadon high-speed press line is engineered for high-precision, high-volume metal forming applications. With a robust welded steel frame and the ability to deliver up to 280 strokes per minute, it ensures stability, accuracy, and consistent production performance. The machine is ideal for stamping operations in industries such as HVAC fin production, can lid manufacturing, and aluminum packaging. With a robust welded steel frame and the ability to deliver up to 280 strokes per minute, it ensures stability, accuracy, and consistent production performance. The machine is ideal for stamping operations in industries such as HVAC fin production, can lid manufacturing, and aluminum packaging. The Yadon 125T press line is a complete, modular system, including components such as uncoilers, air feeders, suction stackers, and integrated hydraulic and electric systems. Its energy-efficient design, coupled with precise motion control, makes it suitable for large-scale manufacturing environments where zero-defect output and consistent quality are essential.

State-of-the-Art Manufacturing Facility

Designed for Precision, Built for Performance

Our manufacturing plant spans 5,000 sq. ft., strategically designed to house up to 5 high-speed press lines. The layout ensures smooth material flow, optimized production efficiency, and adherence to the highest quality standards.

The facility is equipped with modern infrastructure, dedicated rotor and stator welding zones, and specialized press tool design and manufacturing areas to support end-to-end stamping operations.
